About Our Company

The Arab Company for Web Design and Internet Services

"Arcoelec" is an Egyptian company specialized in providing integrated software solutions and digital marketing for companies and individuals, as the company provides digital transformation studies, systems analysis, database design, consultancy and training. In 2013, and since the beginning, the company has helped a large number of companies and individuals in digital transformation in Egypt, the Arab world, the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.

Vision

We at Arcolic believe that technology is a treasure much of which has not been discovered yet, and that integrated software solutions can move companies and institutions thousands of steps forward, enable them to better manage their resources, and achieve rapid growth, based on accuracy and speed in performance, and this is what we seek to provide to our customers on the basis of Precisely, integrated software solutions and digital marketing that enable them to dispense with traditional methods of performing tasks, keep pace with technical progress in the world, and make performing tasks, business management and marketing easier, more accurate, faster and more profitable.

Mission

Assisting large and small companies and institutions alike in achieving increased growth, achieving goals and reaping more profits, by providing integrated software solutions that help them in the financial and administrative management of their institutions through a distinguished team of creative and creative programmers and designers, in addition to appropriate software consulting that helps them to Continue to succeed.

Our Story

Our story in Arkolik is like a dream, through ambitious youth who were fascinated by programming and pulled them into its seas, so they wanted to extract from its treasures to change the face of financial and administrative life in companies and institutions, which is what made them start Arkolik by default at the beginning, and it was a correct view of the future as establishing a company on the Internet means That there are no limits to clients and there are no limits to the ideas presented either, which enabled the company to provide its services to clients from various Arab countries since its inception, then the company moved in 2013 to establish its headquarters on the ground in addition to its online work as an Egyptian company, after achieving many successes during The period of its operation as a virtual company since 2004 operating on the B2B system, and has participated throughout its history in the digital transformation of various institutions, whether in the public or private sector.
